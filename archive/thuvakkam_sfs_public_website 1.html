About SFS
What is Sponsor for Success?
SFS is an educational support initiative by Thuvakkam Education focused on helping students continue their education through scholarships, mentoring, emotional support, and continuous monitoring.
Educational barriers
Many students face interruptions due to financial, social, and geographical challenges that prevent them from completing their education.
Financial challenges
Families struggle to support educational expenses including fees, transport, study materials, and digital access tools.
Continuity support
SFS focuses on long-term educational continuity and student wellbeing through sustained monitoring and mentoring.

Financial need
Evaluation of family background, annual income, and existing financial challenges they face.
Verification
