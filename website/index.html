About SFS

What is Sponsor for Success?

SFS is an educational support initiative by Thuvakkam Education, focused on helping students continue their education through scholarships, mentoring, emotional support, and continuous progress monitoring.

Educational barriers
Many students face interruptions due to financial, social, and geographical challenges that prevent them from completing their education.
Financial challenges
Families struggle to cover educational expenses including fees, transport, study materials, and digital access tools needed for learning.
Continuity support
SFS focuses on long-term educational continuity and student wellbeing through sustained monitoring, mentoring, and community engagement.
