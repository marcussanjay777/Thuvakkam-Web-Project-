Transparency

How your donation reaches a student

A simple, accountable path from your contribution to the student's hands.

01
You donate
Your contribution is received into the Thuvakkam Education trust account, which is audited annually.
02
Student matched
The committee matches your contribution to a selected student based on the sponsorship tier.
03
Funds disbursed
Scholarship amount transferred to the school or directly to the student family as required.
04
You are updated
You receive confirmation of disbursement and then quarterly progress updates for Champion tier and above.
